Welcome to The Clean Plate Club

What is the Clean Plate Club?

The Clean Plate Club is a club dedicated to advocating for healthier eating habits and for reducing food waste. We believe that wasting food is bad for both our health and planet.

Why should you join?

By joining The Clean Plate Club, you’re helping to reduce food waste. You’ll also be joining a community of like-minded people who care about their health and the environment.

How do I join?

Joining The Clean Plate Club is easy! All you have to do is sign up with your name, email address, and phone number. You’ll be sent an email with more information on how to get started and how to become a part of our community.

What are the benefits?

The Clean Plate Club provides members with discounts to food-related businesses. We also provide resources to help members create healthier eating habits and reduce food waste. Members also have access to exclusive events and activities.

How can I support the cause?

One way to show your support for The Clean Plate Club is to spread the word. Talk to your friends, family, and co-workers about our cause and encourage them to join. You can also support us through donations or volunteering.

Where can I find more information?

If you have any questions or would like to learn more about The Clean Plate Club, please visit our website at www.thecleanplateclub.org.

Thank you for your interest!

We appreciate your interest in The Clean Plate Club and look forward to having you join us!
